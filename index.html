About Dropout Code

We’re an indie games studio based in Türkiye, initially founded by two friends, to be expanded upon later in order to reach wider audiences by creating games and experiences of (hopefully) increasingly greater quality.

Let us tell you a story...


Long before the birth of Dropout Code, there was a far more little game studio, developing and distributing mobile games, and experimenting all along the way.

And now with its rebirth, we're able to create games of increasingly greater production value, all while still pushing the limits of fun and player satisfaction.
